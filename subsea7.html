Subsea 7 Edition

2026 World Cup prediction league

Submit your group-stage score picks and knockout winners before the tournament starts. As official results are entered, the leaderboard updates automatically so everyone can track who is getting closest.

Game Rules

  • Each player submits a single prediction entry before the tournament begins.
  • Enter exact scores for the group matches and pick winners for the knockout rounds.
  • Once confirmed, the prediction is locked for the entire tournament.

Group Stage

  • Correct result: 2 points. Exact score: extra 2 points.
  • The maximum available from one group-stage match is 4 points.
  • Best third-placed teams are ranked by points, goal difference and goals scored.

Knockout Scoring

  • Round of 32: 2 points, Round of 16: 3, Quarter-finals: 4, Semi-finals: 5.
  • Correct finalists earn 6 points each, the correct champion earns 10 points.
  • The correct third-place pick earns 4 points, and the leaderboard updates as results come in.
